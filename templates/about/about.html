About Us

Welcome to Script

We are a group of success driven people with exceptional skills imparting quality enriched education.

We stand out for the quality of education that we impart. Our team of experts offer exceptional guidance to passionate individuals for accomplishing their goals. Our exemplary community serves as a platform for you to learn, grow and establish yourself. We will help you to Learn, Leap and Lead

Established on May, 2019

Script was established with the aim to help passionate individuals achieve their goals. Our excellent panel of experts guide you in the right path. Script is open to all individuals and offers extensive support for beginners to come up in different fields of Technology.

Our Aim

Script aspires to promote education in an interactive manner so that individuals can learn something new and interesting. We aim to build a community by bringing together professionals and students with the desire to learn more and conquer heights.

Our Vision

We aim to build a large student community reaching a wide range of individuals. Our vision is to impart knowledge in various technical fields to people with the fire to learn.We serve as a platform for enthusiastic learners to grow and establish themselves.
