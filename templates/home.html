Our Aim

Learn, Leap and Lead

Script aspires to promote education in an interactive manner so that individuals can learn something new and interesting. We aim to build a community by bringing together professionals and students with the desire to learn more and conquer heights.

Script Foundation App now on Playstore!

Here is what we bring to you !

Opportunities to grow

We are a group of success driven people with exceptional skills and diverse experiences. Our team of experts impart quality enriched education to individuals with the urge to learn.
Internship offerings

Our community comes up with immense internship opportunities to help you shape your future and attain your goals. We lead you through the right path into the right hands so that you can polish your skills and use them effectively and profitably.
Regular Meetups

We hold regular meetups for our chapters where we discuss the latest and upcoming technologies, thereby learning about the same. Our community keeps on expanding and these meetups also serve as a symbol of our progress.
Educational Events

We offer numerous educational activities including workshops, webinars and bootcamp. We also promote and reward the talent of enthusiastic individuals through our various ventures like hackathons. We are driven by the motive to spread quality education to passionate individuals.
Heuristic learning approach

We serve as a platform for you to discover yourself through exploring your talents in the technical field. We put forward immense opportunities for you to gain knowledge by practical experience.
A community to learn, grow and prosper

We are here to help you learn, leap and lead. Amongst like minded people, you will get to learn from others and discover your immense potential. We will help you to grow and achieve your goals.
